Best Practices For Form Design

First of all, this article summarizes the various elements of the web form very well. It is a good source to learn from. In the article, the author introduced the key elements for the forms and compared the matters that should be pay attention to when making the survey form, such as how to arrange, the correct size of the input box, and how(where) to put the tips .

In fact, all the author's points are to explain the most important purpose of an interaction design, which is user experience. The most important thing in interactive design is to make users feel smooth and comfortable. Filling out a form with a clear organization will make users feel happy and satisfied. If registering a website requires filling in very tedious information, then users are likely to abandon registration or feel annoyed. Or, messy typesetting and incorrect auto-fill will cause unnecessary trouble for users such as missing information or filling in wrong information. The author summarized the purpose of web form design in a very concise sentence - less is more.
