10 Intriguing Photographs to Teach Close Reading and Visual Thinking Skills

In the article, the author discussed the use of pictures to cultivate people's ability to read carefully. At first glance, these pictures are photos of people and life. But when you look closely and zoom in on the details of the study, you will feel the story inside and what happened. We can look at photos like detectives to collect evidence and repeat this training process consciously. At the same time, we can also ask ourselves questions, but each person's answer may be different, because everyone's perception of the world is also different. Through continuous practice (such practice is very interesting) we can improve our detailed reading ability.
