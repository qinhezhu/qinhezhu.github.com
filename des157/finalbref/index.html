Design Strategies

The overall style of my work is concise and clear. Everyone can guess that my website is about traveling based on the fly tickets they see. I use the blue font because it is the color of the sky, the white overlay is clouds, and the lavender background is the California sunset.

Interaction Strategies

After opening the webpage, the user needs to select one of the tickets, and there will be a hover when choosing a ticket. After clicking the ticket, an overlay will appear showing the photo and location. Clicking on the photo with the mouse will show the character time and some memories. Then click the close sign to close the overlay and choose another ticket. Basically, the appearance of every element requires the user's operation to achieve.

Experience goals

I hope users will remember their travel experience after exploring my webpage.
